About Algeria

Algeria, or the Algerian Republic, is the largest country in Africa. It stretches from the Mediterranean Sea to the Sahara Desert, with a rich mix of cultures and beautiful landscapes. Its diverse regions include mountains, plains, and vast deserts.

Algeria has a long history, with ancient Roman, Byzantine, and Islamic sites, as well as cultural and artistic heritage that attracts visitors from around the world.

Arabic is the official language, and French is widely spoken. From the coastal cities to the Sahara, Algeria offers stunning views, ancient architecture, and unique natural sites.

Explore Algeria through its landscapes, historical monuments, traditions, and cuisine.

The country combines modern cities with traditional life, offering unforgettable experiences for tourists.
