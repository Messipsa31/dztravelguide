Tourist Activities in Algeria

Introduction

Algeria offers an incredible diversity of landscapes and climates, with a wide range of tourist activities suitable for all ages, from mountain adventures to desert excursions and water sports.

Mountain Activities

  • Hiking in the Atlas Mountains or Djurdjura.
  • Rock climbing in the Sahara mountains, cliffs, and peaks.
  • Mountain biking in gorges and rugged terrains.
  • Wildlife and nature observation.

Desert Activities

  • 4x4 excursions in dunes and oases.
  • Visiting sand palaces and ancient ksars.
  • Desert safaris (Timimoun, Béni Abbès).
  • Camping and trekking in the heart of the Sahara.

Maritime Activities

  • Diving in coastal areas like Jijel, Tipaza, and Zeralda.
  • Surfing, kitesurfing, and windsurfing along Algeria’s beaches.
  • Swimming in coves and lagoons.
  • Boat trips and sport fishing.

Sports & Adventure

  • Rock climbing and canyoning in the Rif and Aurès Mountains.
  • Rafting in rivers and streams.
  • Paragliding in mountain and coastal regions.
  • Hiking and mountain biking for all levels.

Travel Tips

  • Plan your itinerary before departure.
  • Respect local traditions and the environment.
  • Do not venture alone in desert or mountainous areas.
  • Interact with locals for practical advice and guidance.
